Octalysis — 8 Core Drives

Octalysis là framework phân tích motivation trong game design, chia thành 8 Core Drives giải thích tại sao người chơi hành động. Mỗi mechanic trong game hoạt động vì kích hoạt ít nhất 1 drive. White Hat (CD1–5) tạo cảm giác tích cực, bền vững. Black Hat (CD6–8) tạo urgency nhưng dễ gây burnout. Target: ≥60% White Hat / ≤40% Black Hat per week.

⬜ White Hat (CD1–5) — 60% target
⬛ Black Hat (CD6–8) — Max 40%
Player Psychology · Bartle Taxonomy adapted

6 Player Archetypes

Dựa trên nghiên cứu của Richard Bartle (1996) phân loại 4 player types: Achiever ♦, Explorer ♠, Socializer ♥, Killer ♣. Trong fashion games, "Killer" → "Competitor" (driven by status), thêm 2 archetypes đặc thù: Collector (wardrobe completionism) và Bargain Hunter (deal-seeking conversion).

High-Spend Mechanics Across Industry

Hell Event & Premium Event Case Studies

Hell events = the most expensive limited-time events in fashion gacha games. Reliably double baseline revenue. The node/milestone structure creates escalating sunk-cost commitment.

🔥
Why "Hell" Events Work

5 reinforcing mechanisms: (1) Sunk cost via nodes — walking away wastes prior investment. (2) FOMO — suits never return after 2 reruns. (3) Variable reinforcement — random drops at each pull. (4) Currency obfuscation — $ → gems → pulls hides real cost. (5) Social pressure — incomplete suits are visible to peers.
